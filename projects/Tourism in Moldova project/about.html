Republic of Moldova

31 years of Independence

Located in Eastern Europe, bordered by Romania to the west and Ukraine to the north, south and east , Moldova is a country with a rich but little known tourist potential. The pride of Moldova are the natural landscapes, in some enigmatic places; they are the historical places and fortifications that carry the breath of history and that of victorious battles; there are the wines and cellars that compete with the French ones; they are the churches and monasteries in the construction of which the soul was deposited as a result, becoming places of pilgrimage and healing of the soul; the pride of Moldova are the native people who create the spirit of hospitality in our small country. After gaining independence, the Republic of Moldova kept its beautiful holidays and traditions, delighting with its delicious and most importantly ecological national cuisine, as well as with Moldovan customs and culture. Do not hesitate to come to the Republic of Moldova to know it in all its splendor.
